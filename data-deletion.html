Delete Your Street Yak Data

You can request full deletion of your personal data at any time using either method below.

Option 1 — Delete in the App

  1. Open Street Yak on your device and sign in
  2. Tap Menu (burger icon, bottom-left)
  3. Go to Profile → Settings
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm — your account and all associated data will be permanently and immediately deleted

Option 2 — Email Request

Send an email to hardyjames86@hotmail.co.uk with:

  • Subject: Data Deletion Request
  • The email address or username linked to your account

We will process your request and confirm deletion within 30 days.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account credentials and profile information
  • Your home address and postcode
  • All posts, comments, and media you have submitted
  • Your social login connections (Facebook, Google, Apple)
  • Your push notification device tokens
  • Your community memberships and preferences
